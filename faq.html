FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ's)

Some questions that you might have in mind.

GENERAL

What does an Errand Runner do?
They run errands for people who are unable to–typically seniors. Most of their jobs are normal everyday tasks, such as getting groceries, stopping at the post office and more.
How to become an errand runner ?
Thank you for your interest in becoming an errand runner,you can get in touch here to become one of us. We process registrations on a rolling basis and will contact you an interview to confirm your eligibility to start tasking. Feel free to visit the get in touch Resources for questions regarding the joining the process.

COMMON

What hours do you have to work?
As an errand runner, you set your schedule. Generally most of your jobs will be in the daytime, but there might be some nighttime work if you’d like.
How much can you get paid?
Errand runners typically earn buy the number of tasks completed and each task is paid hourly.
Can you work part-time?
You can work as much or as little as you’d like. You don’t have to be a full-time errand runner to make good money or get jobs. Running errands can be a second job, a way to make extra cash, or whatever you’d like. You decide.

CARRIER

Do you need any training or certifications?
None. Are you a compassionate person who loves to help others? Good, you have what it takes to be an errand runner.
Is there really a lot of work in this field?
With the current kenyan population and with the number expected to double by 2030. people will continue to age, and many will become unable to complete errands. So what does that mean for you? A lot of potential work as an errand runner.
